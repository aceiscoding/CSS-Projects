I am currently writing my second book called “Particle Ruminations”, a sequel to my first book—Silent Contemplations

A few years after I get buried on the ground, no one will remember me. No one will see what I’ve done, no one will admire what I’ve accomplished, no one will know what books I’ve read, what food I like to eat, what music I dance to in the morning. Unless I become a superstar or an inventor or discoverer of something profound, my life would be lost in time. The stories I’ve told about myself would also disappear from the mouths of those who heard them.

This is my daily meditation. It humbles me and it brings me closer to the present moment. My vision is to live simply, humbly, and truthfully; indifferent to things that don’t make a difference, not wasting any moment or any opportunity to grow and to improve.

I am proud of how I built my life so far. I want to continue living a better, happier life while extending my knowledge further in my field so that I can be of service to those who need me. For that is the only memory I can leave behind that will last forever; not in the minds or mouths but through consequential actions.

When I get old, I want to look back to these memories and be reminded of the joyful moments of my life.
